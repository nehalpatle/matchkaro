We keep your personal information only as long as needed for legitimate business purposes and as allowed by the law. If you stop using our services, closing your account will make your profile invisible to others. Inactivity for two years will lead to automatic account closure. After this, your personal data is deleted as follows:

Safety Retention Window: :
  • For three months after account closure, we retain your data to investigate unlawful or harmful conduct. This is based on our interest and potential third-party protection.
Data Retention Obligations:
  • Limited data is kept to fulfill legal retention obligations, such as transaction data for 10 years for tax and accounting compliance.
  • Traffic data/logs may be kept for one year as required by law.

Legitimate Interest:

Outstanding Issues or Disputes:
  • Data may be retained if there's an ongoing issue, claim, or dispute that requires it, based on our legitimate interest or legal obligations.
