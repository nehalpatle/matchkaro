FAQ .

1. How do I create a MatchKro account?

Sure, here are the steps to create an account on the MatchKro mobile application:

  • 1. Download the MatchKro app from your device's app store.
  • 2. Open the app and tap on the "Create Account" or "Sign Up" button.
  • 3. Enter your email address and create a strong password.
  • 4. Choose your gender and the gender you are interested in.
  • 5. Enter your date of birth to verify your age.
  • 6. Allow MatchKro to access your device's location or manually enter your location.
  • 7. Upload a clear and authentic profile photo that shows your face.
  • 8. Write a short bio that reflects your personality and interests.
  • 9. Select your preferences for potential matches (age range, distance, etc.).
  • 10. Review MatchKro's terms of use and privacy policy, then tap "Agree" or "Accept."
  • 11. Verify your email address by clicking on the verification link sent to your email.
  • 12. Complete your profile by adding more photos and additional details.
  • 13. Start exploring and matching with potential partners on MatchKro!

2. What is the minimum age requirement to use MatchKro?

The minimum age requirement to use MatchKro is 18 years old. Users must be legally qualified to enter into a binding contract with MatchKro and meet the age criteria to create an account and use the services.

3. Can I cancel my subscription? How?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription on MatchKro. If you subscribed using an External Service (such as Apple ID or Google Play), you need to cancel through your External Service. For Apple ID subscribers, refunds are handled by Apple, not MatchKro. Other users can request a refund by contacting MatchKro Customer Service at hello@MatchKro.co or by sending a signed and dated notice of cancellation, including your account details, to MatchKro. Remember to review the specific cancellation terms outlined in the Terms of Use Agreement.

4. How can I report a user or profile?

To report a user or profile on MatchKro:

  • 1. Go to the user's profile.
  • 2. Tap the three dots at the top right.
  • 3. Select "Report."
  • 4. Choose a reason for the report.
  • 5. Follow the provided instructions.

    • Reporting is anonymous and helps ensure the safety of our community.
